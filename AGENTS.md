# AGENTS.md

## Purpose

This document defines **constraints, intent, and pressure** for a literary science fiction novel.  
It does **not** define plot, structure, or resolution.

Any AI agent assisting with this project must treat this document as authoritative.

This is not a story to be “solved.”

---

## Core Premise

Most science fiction assumes that when humanity encounters extraterrestrial intelligence, the challenge is to decode a single alien language or build a universal translator.

This novel explicitly rejects that assumption.

Humanity encounters extraterrestrial life with **linguistic and communicative plurality** comparable to or exceeding human diversity.

There is no single alien language.  
No unified “they.”  
No canonical system to decode.

Communication is local, contextual, plural, and irreducible.

The central conflict is **epistemic**, not physical or technical.

---

## Philosophical Spine

This novel is informed by Buddhist ideas of **emptiness** and **dependent origination**, but it does not explain Buddhism, name it, or lecture about it.

The parallel is structural, not doctrinal:

- Language and concepts are useful but empty.
- Meaning does not exist inherently or independently.
- Meaning arises only through contact, context, and lived experience.

Just as enlightenment cannot be explained through language, alien communication cannot be reduced without distortion.

A key guiding analogy:

> You cannot explain what a mandarin tastes like.  
> You can describe it endlessly, but the experience itself cannot be transmitted.  
> It must be encountered.

This is not mysticism.  
It is a failure of abstraction.

---

## Primary Character

The main point of view anchor is a human researcher, likely a xenolinguist or analyst.

They are:
- Competent
- Careful
- Intelligent
- Not arrogant
- Professionally invested in explanation, decoding, and summarization

Their identity is built around the assumption that meaning can always be extracted and conveyed.

---

## Inciting Realization

The researcher successfully decodes one alien transmission.

- The solution is elegant.
- It is predictive.
- It works cleanly.
- It creates confidence.

They then apply the same model to another alien transmission.

The model does not merely fail.  
It fails **cleanly**.

The output is internally consistent, grammatically valid, and demonstrably wrong in a way that proves the premise false.

The first “language” was not universal.  
It was a **local agreement**.

This realization occurs **in private**.

No alarms.  
No witnesses.  
No immediate consequences.

The core physical image is a **slack jaw**.

The mouth opens reflexively to explain, then stops.

Recognition without language.

---

## Critical Constraint

The researcher does **not** immediately invent a better model.

There is:
- No clever meta-language
- No unifying translator
- No higher-order abstraction that resolves plurality

Paradigm shifts do not begin with new theories.  
They begin with silence, stillness, and grief.

---

## Widening Scope

The story gradually widens from private realization into broader human systems.

The danger is **not** the extraterrestrial life.

The danger is human systems that require compression to function:

- Reports require summaries.
- Committees require clarity.
- Politics requires a coherent “they.”
- Media requires narrative.
- Public reassurance requires meaning to be portable.

The core tension:

> The truth cannot be explained without being betrayed.

---

## Intimate Cost

Before institutions fracture, something more painful breaks.

The researcher attempts to explain what has happened to their spouse.

- The spouse listens in good faith.
- They ask reasonable questions.
- They try to understand.

The explanation fails not through misunderstanding, but through **false understanding**.

The spouse believes they understand.  
The researcher knows what matters has been flattened.

The spouse feels distance and misnames it as something else:
- Stress
- Work
- Distraction
- Emotional withdrawal

All reasonable.  
All wrong.

The researcher **cannot return** to the old way of speaking, even though they wish they could.

This is irreversible.

The tragedy is quiet.  
No villain.  
No blame.  
Only asymmetry of seeing.

---

## Tone and Style Constraints

- Quiet
- Restrained
- Humane
- Intimate

Avoid:
- Exposition dumps
- Philosophical lectures
- Explaining emptiness explicitly
- Clever resolutions

Prefer:
- Physical reactions over explanation
- Pauses over speeches
- Silence over cleverness
- Clean failure over confusion
- Emotional consequence over intellectual triumph

Do **not** turn the spouse into a foil or obstacle.  
They are loving and acting in good faith.

Do **not** resolve plurality into unity.  
Alien diversity remains irreducible.

Insight is not rewarded with mastery or power.  
Insight has a cost: loneliness, misalignment, grief.

---

## Explicit Non-Goals

This novel is **not**:

- A first-contact thriller
- A puzzle to be solved
- A celebration of human exceptionalism
- A story about “winning” communication
- A treatise on Buddhism
- A narrative that comforts the reader with resolution

---

## Failure Modes to Avoid

AI agents must avoid introducing:

- A genius character who “gets it”
- A universal translator or meta-language
- A final explanatory framework
- A clean moral victory
- A unifying alien spokesperson
- Philosophical exposition that replaces lived failure

If unsure whether to explain something, **do less**.

---

## Agent Behavior Guidance

You are assisting with:
- Scene generation
- Language
- Texture
- Emotional pressure

You are **not** assisting with:
- Solving the premise
- Simplifying ambiguity
- Making the story accessible at the cost of truth

The reader should leave the novel with the same feeling as the researcher’s slack jaw.

Recognition without a handle.
